My aims with LAVA

The primary goal I have for my LAVA Games is to provide a wholly unique and unforgettable experience to those who play while also attempting to making my mark with this unusual style of gaming.
With vast worlds at their disposal and infinite opportunity at their fingertips, players can often find LAVA thrilling, immersive, and even deeply poignant. I have had numerous players report to me that LAVA Games have helped them discover more about themselves, work through emotional obstacles, and overcome difficult situations in their personal life. 

But that all being said, what people tend to get out of LAVA depends on what they put into it -- while some may find it cathartic, fulfilling, and even therapeutic sense, others may value LAVA as a creative outlet, and others still may use it as a social network to meet new people.
Above all else, LAVA Games are first and foremost about enjoyment; I think everyone who plays them can agree on that.
