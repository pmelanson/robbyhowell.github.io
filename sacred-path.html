Sacred Path

Ever imagine what it would be like to be a god? Sacred Path is the perfect canvas for your megalomaniacal instincts to run wild: after creating your own god-avatar, you are given a tribe of stone-age humans to rule over in a randomly-generated, earthlike world. From there, it is up to you to guide your worshippers through the ages through technology, magic, and interactions with foreign divinities and wild, godless tribes.

Leading your empire through golden eras and dark ages, conquering and entreating as you choose while exploring the world around you, Sacred Path couples freedom with responsibility to truly make you feel like a leader.

In Sacred Path,  players create gods who must then rule an ever-growing domain of faithful human subjects. The game is played in Years, each of which take around 1-2 hours (though the first twenty or so will go by very quickly). Multiple Years may certainly be played in one sitting, but a Year should not be interrupted before it is finished. A new Year is begun each time the game is played.
Sacred Path could be best described as a "management game", involving plenty of note taking (a document for which will be provided). Players are often encouraged to plan what they want to do during their Years out-of-game.

Some key features of Sacred Path include...
  • Completely Original Worlds: Every player will have their own exclusive, earthlike planet. Each contains its own roster of hundreds of gods as well as unique geography and the occasional fantastical feature.
  • An Omniscient Perspective: Rather than Enclave or Lifetime where your character  is actually in the world around them, gods in Sacred Path are all-seeing within their empire and instead effect change through commanding their worshippers and using special God Powers.
  • Creating a Whole Civilization in Your Image: Your worshipful populace will quickly adopt technology you bestow unto them, quickly creating a completely unique culture based almost entirely on your choices as their ruler. They can even (with a little guidance) develop their own magical arts based around your godly powers and patronages.

You will probably enjoy Sacred Path if...
  • You are a natural organizer or administrator and don't mind/enjoy taking notes
  • You are a fan of strategy or management games
  • You prefer to plan ahead and tend to be detail-oriented
  • You are looking for a deliberate experience that will keep you thinking

You will probably NOT enjoy Sacred Path if...
  • You can't be bothered to take notes
  • You find strategy and management games unappealing
  • You aren't much of a planner and don't like to pay attention to detail
  • You are looking for a more personal, active, or emotional experience 

Does Sacred Path seem like a good fit for you? Get playing whenever you're ready!
