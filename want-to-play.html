How to Start Playing

Getting started with LAVA couldn't be easier: simply email me at robbyworks80092@gmail.com or text/call 617-610-2871 to let me know that you are interested, and we can set a date to start! 

LAVA Games can be played either in-person (if you are in the Boston Area) or over Skype/Google Hangouts. I also encourage that new players set a time to talk with me over phone or Skype so we can get acquainted and I can answer questions before we dive headfirst into games.
This is especially important if you are interested in Enclave, as before that game in particular can be played I will need to share a bunch of Google Docs with you and help you create a character. The other two (Lifetime and Sacred Path) are much easier to jump right into.

If none of my current games are catching your eye but you definitely want to be involved in LAVA, I would recommend checking out more of my Commissioned Work or contacting me to learn about Beta Testing!

There is no set age range for LAVA Games, however I should note that children below 10 years of age are less likely to get as much out of them (though some precocious children as young as 8 have thoroughly enjoyed playing).
Apart from this caveat, LAVA Games have been appreciated by players from 11 to 45 years of age. Please bear in mind that all players under the age of 18 require the explicit approval of their legal parent or guardian in order to participate.

Reimbursement

Each LAVA Game has a different payment model that will be further detailed when a player expresses interest in that game. But never fear, all LAVA Games offer a number of free sessions to ensure that you enjoy the service before you are required to pay for it:
  • Enclave: The first three Missions the player participates in will be free of charge.
  • Lifetime: The first three Turns the player takes will be free of charge.
  • Sacred Path: The first ten Years the player passes will be free of charge.
Once these free trials have elapsed, LAVA Games tend to cost (on average) $15-30 per session, though this is highly variable based on the game being played and how long it is played for. Once again, contact me with further questions.

Credit may also be purchased to be spent on LAVA Games. This must be bought in bulk, but offers a much better deal than paying normally if you are willing to spend a large amount of money up front.
Players can also earn Credit through introduce new players to LAVA or contributing in other ways as they may arise, allowing those who get involved to effectively earn free LAVA if they participate.

Player Responsibilities

Before play begins, players will be asked both to read a document on Rights & Responsibilities and to fill out a survey detailing their Preferences. This is to ensure that I give the player as enjoyable a LAVA experience as possible and will not be shared with anyone else unless the player is participating in a multiplayer game, in which case some aspects may be made public to ensure their fellows are also aware of their boundaries.
Players who are under the age of 18 must have their parent or guardian sign off on these documents in order for them to be accepted.
